Welcome to Fantasy Hack!

The brand new tool in Fantasy Basketball! By taking into account points scored, assists, and rebounds made (among others) in previous games, our system can reliably predict if your favorite player will be the next game's MVP, or whether or not you should have made that trade in the first place. Think of it as a stock market for your roster. Players projected to do well in the coming game will be much-sought after, while under-performing players will simply take up space.

So if you're trying something new, or just making sure a trade is worth it, stay ahead of the game with Fantasy Hack.
